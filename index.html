Who We Are

At TOTESS Studio, we are passionate about creating digital experiences that make a difference. We are a boutique software and gaming studio currently a solo venture—dedicated to building products that blend beautiful design with meaningful functionality.

Every project we undertake is born from genuine passion and a commitment to excellence. We believe in crafting experiences that not only look great but truly enhance the lives of those who use them. From concept to launch, we pour our heart into every detail.

Our philosophy is simple: we build "Executive Assistants" for your daily life. Whether it's a gaming experience or a health utility, our tools are designed to be calm, intelligent, and respectful of your time.

TasteTurner

The Gamified Kitchen Intelligence Platform

TasteTurner reimagines the diet tracker as a professional culinary service. We replaced the calorie-counting chore with a "Sous Chef" AI that turns ingredients into inspiration.

Core Innovation:

  • Cycle-Syncing Engine: Proprietary nutrition logic adapting meal plans to hormonal phases.
  • Computer Vision QC: "Instant Plate QC" analyzes meals for nutritional density and aesthetics in real-time.
  • Gamified Inventory: Reduces food waste by turning the pantry into an "Inspiration Wheel" challenge.
